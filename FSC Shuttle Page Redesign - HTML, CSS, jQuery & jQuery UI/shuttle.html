The Farmingdale Shuttle

Hello students, faculty, staff, and alumni of Farmingdale State College!

Farmingdale State College is now offering a courtesy shuttle that takes members of the FSC community to and from Laffin Hall, the Aviation Center, and the Farmingdale Long Island Railroad (LIRR).

The shuttle operates throughout the Spring (1/28/17 to 5/19/17) and Fall (8/28/17 to 12/19/17) semesters and is available from Monday to Friday during the day (7 AM - 5 PM) and Monday to Thursday during the evening (after 5 PM).

The shuttle requires the presentation of a Farmingdale State College ID to board.

The Farmingdale shuttle is also wheelchair accessible,
allows food and drink to be brought aboard,
and provides space for passengers who are travelling with luggage.

The shuttle does not operate on Saturdays, Sundays, and days when Farmingdale State College's campus is closed or classes are cancelled.


Do you have a question?

To ask a question or check on the status of the shuttle, please call 631-420-2103.

Additional information about the Farmingdale shuttle can be found in the FAQ.

If You Miss Your Shuttle...

If you miss your shuttle... don't panic!

A taxi service can take you to and from the Farmingdale LIRR and the Farmingdale campus.

Taxi Service Phone Number Fare
Checkers Cab 516-777-1111 $8
Yellow Cab 516-249-6666 $8


Food Venues Around The Farmingdale LIRR

If you're feeling hungry before or after your travels, there are several venues around the Farmingdale LIRR you can visit to get a quick meal or beverage.

The Farmingdale LIRR has a Starbucks that serves coffee and various snacks, as well as a T.A.O Asian Fusion restaurant across from it that serves Asian cuisine.

Several other venues (such as a deli, a library cafe, and a gym) can be found on Main Street, which is about a 15 minute walk from the Farmingdale LIRR.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need a Farmingdale State College ID to board the shuttle?

Yes. All passengers will be asked to present their Farmingdale State College ID before they can board the shuttle.

Do I have to pay to board the Farmingdale shuttle?

No. If you are a Farmingdale State College student, alumni, staff, or faculty member then you will only need to present your FSC ID to board the shuttle.
The courtesy shuttle service is funded by the Student Transportation Fee.

What happens if the shuttle becomes full at a stop?

The shuttle will transport the first set of passengers and then return to the stop to transport the remaining passengers to their destination.

Is the shuttle wheelchair accessible?

Yes. The shuttle is wheelchair accessible.

Can I bring luggage onto the shuttle?

Yes. The shuttle allows its passengers to bring luggage aboard.

Can I bring food and/or a beverage onto the shuttle?

Yes. The shuttle allows food and beverages to brought aboard.

Is the shuttle available on days when the Farmingdale campus is closed or classes are cancelled?

No. The shuttle is not available on days when the Farmingdale campus is closed or classes are cancelled.

How long does the shuttle wait at a stop?

The shuttle waits for 5 minutes at every stop.

How can I check if my shuttle is running late?

To check on the status of the shuttle, please call 631-420-2103.

What can I do if I miss my shuttle?

You can call a Yellow Cab (516-249-6666) or Checkers Cab (516-777-1111) taxi service to take you to your destination for $8 per person.

I have a question that wasn't answered here. What can I do?

If you have any unanswered questions about the Farmingdale shuttle, please call 631-420-2103.
